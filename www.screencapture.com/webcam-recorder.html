    Frequently asked questions

    How can I record my webcam?

    To record a webcam video, follow these steps.

    Step 1. Open the webcam recorder web page.

    Step 2. Choose the webcam recording option.

    Step 3. Allow access to the webcam and start recording.

    Step 4. Download the video to your desktop.

    Our webcam recorder is free of charge, and there are no video length limitations.

    Is it safe to record my webcam online?

    Yes, it’s absolutely safe to use an online webcam recorder. You’ll be the only person who sees the video until you download the file and share it with someone else. We can’t access your browser data, and we don’t copy your videos to a hidden backup file.

    Can I record my webcam with audio?

    Sure! You can record your face and voice simultaneously with our online video recorder. To do this, activate the Webcam and Microphone icons on the webcam recorder website and allow access to them before you start recording.

    Can I record my webcam and desktop?

    Yes, you can capture webcam and desktop simultaneously. Open the Screen Capture web page. Activate the Screen and Webcam icons and start recording. You can drag, resize, or display the webcam image over the recording (picture-in-picture).

    I need a desktop version. Do you have one?

    Yes, you can try the free desktop version of our screen recorder.

    This version has advanced video recording settings like mouse and keyboard effects, support for drawing on videos, and much more. With the desktop screen recorder, you can edit recordings and save them in any convenient format without using third-party software.
