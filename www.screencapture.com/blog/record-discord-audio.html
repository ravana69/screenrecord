How to record Discord audio with Craig

Best for: Great audio quality

OS: Windows, macOS

Price: Free to use

Get Craig

Key features:

  • Two-way communication recording
  • Open-source platform
  • Free chat servers
  • Great reliability
  • High-quality audio streaming
  • Auto synchronization of tracks

Pros:

  • Instant audio access
  • Multiple download formats
  • Stores audio to be downloaded for seven days

Cons:

  • It does not support any phone OS

Craig is an open-source bot that you can use for Discord. It allows easy multi-track or multi-channel recording. When the data you want to record is already available, it will start to record audio for multiple audio tracks. Craig is a bot that permits audio recording from both ends of the communication and creates separate files. It will let the user know the extent of editing needed for both sides of audio files to get better quality. Users can download the audio at the time of recording. The bot promises great security besides offering enhanced reliability. It permits six hours of recording duration followed by seven days of file download. To learn how to record audio on Discord using Craig, here are the steps.

  1. Go to the Craig bot website, log into your Discord account, and click Invite. Then add the bot to your server.
  1. Open the voice channel in which you want to record audio. Type /join and select the channel for recording. Craig will start recording audio.
  1. When the conversation is over, click Stop recording.
  1. You’ll receive a download link with your recording in DM.

How to improve Discord audio

The audio input and output quality on Discord relies on various factors. Buying some good hardware is a piece of apparent advice to improve sound quality. Besides that, you can also change some settings in Discord to get improved results. To cut down background noise from the microphone and discard echo, you can follow these steps.

  1. Start by opening Discord and click on Voice & Video, which you can get from the left sidebar.
  2. Keep scrolling down to get to the Advanced section.
  3. Now, simply shift the toggle that you can find next to Noise Suppression.
  4. The next thing that you will need to do is shift the toggle right next to Echo Cancellation.

If you want to adjust the sensitivity of your microphone, you can follow these steps.

  1. Start by opening Discord.
  2. Click on Voice & Video, which you can find from the sidebar on the left.
  3. Now, you will have to turn on Voice Activity.
  4. Shift the slider that you will find under Input Sensitivity.

Tips for choosing a Discord audio recorder

Right before you opt for one program and decide to make it your go-to recording program, there are specific things that you should try to keep in mind. Let’s have a look at them.

  • Compatibility: The first thing you should think about when choosing a recording program is what device you’ll be using. Some programs are cross-platform and have compatible versions for different operational systems and devices, but most apps are developed for a certain OS. So make sure the program you choose is supported on your device.
  • Features: The features that any program offers you should match your wants. Opting for a program that provides only a few features of your need will be no use. For instance, choosing an audio recording program that does not provide both-way recording might not be useful if you are a gamer and want to record it all. It might be the case that you come across two programs, and both provide more or less the same kind of features. In that case, choose the one that you think can provide you with the best of all features.
  • Usability: You will need to ensure that the software or application you choose is compatible with how you wish to record and is easy to use. Trying to opt for a program with loads of features might make it hard for you to use it correctly.
  • Price: The recorder that you choose should be within your budget. It is always a better option to opt for the one that offers a free trial. It can help you ensure that the program works for all your needs right before you commit. Also, you cannot say that paid programs are always better than free ones. There are loads of free recording programs that work better than the paid ones with better features.
  • Support: Choosing a program that offers support features can help you in the long run. For instance, find out if the program that you choose offers email or live chat support.
  • Reviews: We recommend you reading user reviews on the program before using it. This way, you can learn about the advantages of the program and be aware of the drawbacks.

Frequently asked questions

What is Craig Chat Bot?

Craig Chat Bot acts as a voice channel audio recorder for Discord that supports multi-channel or multi-track recording. It indicates that when Craig starts recording your voice channel from Discord, you can get a separate audio track for each of your speakers. The bot will let you cut, level, or edit each speaker recording.

Does Discord save calls?

No, Discord doesn’t record calls. If you want to save your call, you’ll need to use a third-party recording app. Try Movavi Screen Recorder – it lets you easily capture audio and video on your computer. You can also trim your recording if needed.

How do I record a Discord call?

There is no official way in which you can record a Discord call. If you want to do so, you will need to opt for third-party apps like Craig Bot, Audacity, OBS, etc. You can use Craig Bot to easily record voice calls on Discord Server Voice Channels. Let’s look at the steps to record a Discord call using Craig Bot.

  1. Start by opening the official website of Craig Bot.
  2. Click on the option Invite Craig to Your Discord Server.
  3. You will have to choose the desired Discord server you would like to record.
  4. As you select the server, click on Authorized.
  5. You will have to complete the Recaptcha process.
  6. Open the voice channel in Discord.
  7. Type in :craig:,join in the chatbox.
  8. Click on Send. You will be able to record the calls on this channel.

How do I output OBS audio to Discord?

OBS can provide you with more audio-based settings compared to Discord. So, you might want to record audio using OBS and then output the same to Discord. Let’s look at the steps that will help you with this.

  1. Start by signing in to OBS.
  2. Click on the Audio settings that you can find in the left sidebar.
  3. Scroll down to Advanced.
  4. Under the section of Monitoring Device, choose the audio capturing device.
  5. Now, navigate to the section of Audio Mixer.
  6. Click on Advanced Audio Properties, expand the dropdown menu, and select Audio Monitoring.
  7. Choose Monitor and Output or Monitor Only.
  8. Return to the main page of OBS and click on the plus icon that you can find at the bottom-right of the screen.
  9. Click on Audio Input Capture.
  10. You will have to add Discord as a destination for one of the audio input channels. Click OK.
  11. To connect OBS to the Discord stream, go to Settings and then Stream.
  12. Just paste the Stream key and hit OK.
