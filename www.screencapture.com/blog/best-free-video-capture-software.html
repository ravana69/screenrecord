Free video capture software: Online options

If you have ever wished that you could somehow record a virtual meeting, a training session, or a video call, this is the perfect solution. With these free video recording software options, you can capture the audio and visual of your computer desktop, browser, or even webcam. Once you have your video ready, you can edit and annotate it. The image and sound quality are high-level and HD. Create your own training programs or produce personalized videos for YouTube!

Known for its fast saving and good HD image and sound quality, it is a simple yet professional video capture tool.

A browser extension is another option that you can try for recording the video and audio activity on your screen. Some can be used offline as well as online, adding an extra level of adaptability. Recording, editing, and annotating are made easy with the numerous features available. You can then upload or share them across different platforms – web and mobile. Here, we cover the most popular Chrome extensions, which save automatically to your Google Drive, so you can easily store your creations and access them when you need them.

Conclusion

We hope this article has been helpful in your choice of a video capture program. Sometimes, however, it may be difficult to pick just one service among many. So, here’s a list of points you should pay attention to when choosing a video recorder:

OS: make sure to check if the software works on the system you’re using (Windows, macOS, Android, iOS, etc.)

Features: it’s important to pick software suitable to your needs. Most of the video recorders include basic editing options. However, if you’re a professional, you might want to pay attention to the recorders with a greater assortment of features.

Support: try to find software with accessible customer support that will help you out in case of trouble.

Reviews: check out user reviews before you download any program. Other people may share their experience or problems with the software.
