How to use a built-in WhatsApp video recorder on iOS

Best for: iPhone and iPad users

OS: iOS

iOS offers its own built-in WhatsApp video recorder. The most significant advantage to this program is that it requires no download or purchase – it is included on all iPhones, iPad Touches, and iPads with iOS 11 or higher. It is easy to use and records WhatsApp video calls directly onto your device, with plenty of one-click export options for social media.

Pros:

  • No download required
  • Easy to use
  • No cost
  • One-click export options for social media

Cons:

  • Only available on iOS devices
  • Recorded videos take up space on the device

How to use the built-in WhatsApp video recorder on iOS:

  1. Tap Settings. Next, tap Control Center.
  2. Click the + next to Screen Recording.
  3. Open the Control Center by swiping up from the bottom of your home screen (iPhone 8 or earlier, iOS 11 or earlier) or swiping down from the upper righthand corner of the home screen (iPhone X or later, iPadOS 13 or later).
  4. Long-press the Record Button to begin recording.
  5. Open WhatsApp and make the call.
  6. When you are finished recording, tap the red bar at the top of the screen. Choose Stop.

How to use screen mirroring on iOS

Best for: viewing iOS recordings on a compatible smart TV

OS: iOS

In addition to offering built-in screen recording, iOS also offers screen mirroring. This function allows you to project your iPhone, iPad, or iPod Touch screen on an Apple TV or AirPlay 2-compatible smart TV. With this feature, you can watch your screen recordings on your TV after they have been recorded.

Screen mirroring on iOS is easy to use and requires no download or purchase. It requires that your phone and Apple TV or Airplay 2-compatible smart TV are both logged into the same Wi-Fi network.

Pros:

  • No download or purchase required
  • Free of cost
  • Easy to use
  • Watch screen recordings on smart TV

Cons:

  • Only available on iOS
  • Only works with Apple TV and Airplay 2-compatible smart TVs
  • Requires iOS device and smart TV to be connected to the same Wi-Fi network

How to use screen mirroring on iOS:

  1. Tap Settings on your iOS device. Next, tap Wi-Fi.
  2. Choose the same network that your Apple TV or Airplay 2-compatible smart TV is connected to.
  3. Open the Control Center by swiping up from the bottom of your home screen (iPhone 8 or earlier, iOS 11 or earlier) or swiping down from the upper righthand corner of the home screen (iPhone X or later, iPadOS 13 or later).
  4. Tap Screen Mirroring and select your smart TV from the list.
  5. An AirPlay passcode may appear on your TV screen.
  6. Enter the passcode on your iOS device.
  7. Activate Screen Recording from the Control Center.
  8. Open WhatsApp and make your call.
  9. When your call is finished, tap the red bar at the top of your iPhone screen. Choose Stop.
  10. Open the Control Center again and click Screen Recording.
  11. Tap Screen Mirroring. Next, tap Stop Mirroring.

How to use screen mirroring on Android

Best for: viewing Android recordings on compatible smart TVs

OS: Android

Like iOS, Android offers a screen mirroring function. This function is not available with iOS or Windows products, but most other smart TVs are compatible with Android screen mirroring.

The screen mirroring function is built-in on Android OS 5.0 or later, so there is no need to download an app. The Android device and the smart TV need to be linked to the same Wi-Fi network to mirror.

Pros:

  • No download necessary
  • Free to use
  • Very user-friendly

Cons:

  • Not compatible with iOS or Windows TVs
  • Android device and smart TV must be on the same Wi-Fi network
  • Not available on Android OS older than 5.0

How to use screen mirroring on Android:

  1. Go to Settings and turn off Power Saving Mode.
  2. Tap Apps and then tap Google Play Services.
  3. Tap Permissions.
  4. Next to Microphone, move the slider to On.
  5. Go to WiFi and make certain that the Android device is connected to the same network as the smart TV.
  6. Activate the screen recording program you wish to use, like AZ Screen Recorder.
  7. Open the Google Home app and choose the device you wish to cast to.
  8. Choose Cast My Screen. Next, tap Cast Screen.
  9. When you are finished recording, press Stop on the screen recording program overlay.
  10. Go back to the Google Home app and click your device.
  11. Tap Stop Mirroring.
