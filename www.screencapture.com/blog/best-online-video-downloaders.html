Online URL video downloaders: Our top 18

1. SaveFrom.net

Best for: downloading TikTok videos

SaveFrom.net works directly in your browser and lets you download videos from a URL. It is a no-frills piece of software. Using this tool begins with copying and pasting the address of your video into the toolbar at the top of the page. From there, you click the arrow to proceed. You can download the media file either from the SaveFrom.net Helper or by downloading it in your browser. That said, if you use the SaveFrom.net Helper, you are able to download files in more formats than you would if you downloaded via your browser. The SaveFrom.net Helper also allows you to download video from a link in full HD or 4K, and you can use the SaveFrom.net Helper in browsers like Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Opera.

By using SaveFrom.net, you can download clips from some of the most popular video sites like YouTube, Vimeo, Daily Motion, Reddit, and Facebook. SaveFrom.net also contains some helpful frequently asked questions, which you can access here.

Pros:

  • Can download high-quality videos;
  • Allows saving videos from many popular sites.

Cons:

  • Has a lot of ads.

2. Catchvideo.net

Best for: saving videos to Android devices

Catchvideo.net, like SaveFrom.net, contains a simple toolbar where you can insert the link to any video from any site. After clicking the “Catch!” button, you have the ability to save your video in various forms, including MP4, WebM, or 3GP. Catchvideo.net also offers an extension for your browser so that you can download more quickly as you are browsing the internet. Catchvideo.net can help you get videos from YouTube, Facebook, Vimeo, Daily Motion, and a wide range of additional video websites.

Catchvideo.net makes it clear, however, that videos subject to copyright (like Vevo videos and others containing music) cannot be fetched by using its tool. In addition, Catchvideo.net offers several other useful tools, including a Facebook downloader for Android, an Android utility to easily convert a URL to video and audio, and a tool for mixing HD YouTube videos (available on Windows and Linux). You can find more information on these other tools by visiting Catchvideo.net’s website.

Pros:

  • Free online video downloader for PC and macOS;
  • Allows downloading videos in various formats.

Cons:

  • The highest supported video resolution is 720p.

3. Converto.io

Best for: saving videos as audio files

Converto.io is another tool to download clips through a link on the internet. It describes itself as “the easiest way to download and convert YouTube to MP3 or MP4.” Converto.io prides itself on its ease of use. All users need to do is copy a YouTube URL, paste it into Converto.io, and click the “Convert” button to get their video. Converto.io allows users to download and convert any YouTube video to MP3 or MP4 formats without having to download and install any special software. The service is also helpful in that you can edit the file name, ID3 tags, specific excerpts from the video, and the video quality before downloading. If you are saving a video in MP3 format, Converto.io also automatically adds album art to the file.

Pros:

  • Very easy to use;
  • Can save files in MP4 and MP3 formats.

Cons:

  • Too many ads on the website.

4. AmoyShare’s Free YouTube Downloader

Best for: downloading playlists

Created by AmoyShare, Free YouTube Downloader is another web-based app. Here, users immediately see a toolbox where they copy and paste YouTube URLs. From there, users can play the video or save it directly to their computer. Free YouTube Downloader offers users a bevy of features and flexibility. While Free YouTube Downloader is clearly focused on YouTube, users can also get videos from over 1,000 websites – including Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram.

Pros:

  • Can download multiple clips from a YouTube playlist;
  • Allows downloading from many sites by link.

Cons:

  • Downloads YouTube playlists to MP4 or MP3 files only.

5. DistillVideo

Best for: downloading video with a link on Linux devices

DistillVideo is another app that you can use for free. Users simply copy and paste a video or audio URL into the toolbox and are led to a screen where they can download the target media file. From there, the video is presented in different formats, including MP4, WebM, and 3GP. You can also remove the sound from the video while processing. DistillVideo doesn’t require you to download any software to your computer. Everything is processed on the cloud, and DistillVideo does not contain any spyware. DistillVideo works on any browser, whether you are using Windows, Mac, or Linux. If you need help using DistillVideo, the creators have released a comprehensive list of frequently asked questions here.

Pros:

  • The website is very easy to navigate;
  • Allows removing sound from videos.

Cons:

  • Supports converting videos with duration up to 16 minutes into an MP3 file.

Conclusion

As you can see, there are plenty of great options if you want to download a video from a website online. There is definitely a tool out there that will suit your needs, whether you want an installable program or to use any video downloader online.

If you’re not sure which app or tool to use, take the following factors into consideration:

  • Features: Make sure the video downloader you're using has the right features to suit your needs, such as the ability to download videos in bulk or support for downloading videos from sites that are not on the app’s list.
  • Compatibility: Make sure the video downloader you're using is compatible with your operating system and devices.
  • Ease of use: Consider how easy the video downloader is to use. Some video downloaders have a learning curve, while others are very user-friendly.
  • Pricing: If you want a free video downloader, make sure it doesn't have any hidden costs. If you're willing to pay for a video downloader, compare the prices of different apps and choose the one that offers the best value for money.
